FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions

If you don’t find your answer, Please contact us or Leave a Message, we’ll be more than happy to assist you.

Q1.How CoLink is helpful for us ?

CoLink will help you to interact easily with your seniors and will help you to grow together.

Q2.How to send request for Notes and Books ?

Simply , Go to the profile of your senior and send request for book/notes which will show in their profile.

Q3.How to send text message to someone ?

Go to the profile and "Message" option will show there , click on "message" and send text.
